Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and breakdowns, and maintaining your account. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account at any time, which permanently removes all your stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the app's support channels.
